The Project

As a developer, I - of course - need my homepage. I normally don't know what to put there, but I wrote a view paragraphs. More interesting to me is the build process. Thus, the content is not perfect.

Technologies

I wanted to write a single page application. I did this with vanilla.js and W3.CSS. The different pages are html files that dynamically get loaded when a page is selected. The homepage is very lightweight and uses no frameworks besides an Icon-font. A newer feature is the domain detection, as I wanted the title of the page to depend on the url that is used.

Status

The page is live and can be viewd at stousn.at and stefanreip.at (Mind the different titles and headings). Currently only content maintenance is done.
